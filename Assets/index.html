    About Us

    Selection of delicious and unique Bakerz Bite cakes are suitable for all occasions, whether you're celebrating and need a birthday cake or you're ordering in a treat for the office. Bakerz Bite offers a wide range of baked goods made fresh in-store everyday. The bakery chain offers more than 300 different kinds of baked goods, including artisan pastries, gourmet cakes and desserts, and handcrafted beverages. The brand takes pride in sourcing and using carefully selected ingredients and promises to bring freshness and quality to it's customers.
